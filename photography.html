Creative Work

Photography

Capturing moments, telling stories through the lens since 2019

I started photography in 2019 when I bought my first camera — a Canon 550D with an 18-55mm kit lens and a 50mm prime lens. My initial goal was to create videos for my YouTube channel, but I quickly fell in love with still photography alongside it.

In December 2024, I upgraded to a Sony a6400 with a 50mm prime lens and 18-55mm kit lens, which I've used to cover numerous programs and events at my university clubs. As Assistant Media & Publication Head for BAUST CSE Society and BAUST Computer Club over the last 3 years, and as a photographer for BAUST Photography Eye for 6 months, I've had the privilege of documenting campus life, cultural events, and everyday beauty.

Explore more of my work on Purnottaa Photography.
